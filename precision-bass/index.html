ORIGIN

In early 1950's development of amplification for musical instruments made double bass hard to hear at the live gigs. Precision Bass, invented by Leo Fender, was designed to overcome this problem. It was the first electric bass to earn widespread attention and use, remaining among the best-selling and most-imitated electric basses with considerable effect on the sound of popular music.

CONQUEST

During it's glorious history sound of Fender precision bass was used on many classical and significant records in almost any genres from jazz and funk to extreme metal and grindcore. It's simple and literally "precise" construction made it multipurpose tool that is easy to use, perfectly sits in the mix and gives you the sound that became a golden standard for years.

BENEFITS

SOUND

Precision bass gives you get fundamental fat bass tone that not lost amongst other instruments and always hits the target.

SIMPLICITY

With only two knobs, one splitted pickup, thick and wide neck Precision bass is perfect decision for bass beginners as well as for professional players.

SHAPE

Contour form factor of Precision Bass is ergonomic, well balanced and comfortable to hold almost in any position.
